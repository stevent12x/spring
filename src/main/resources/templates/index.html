Our History

In it's current state, Theorems and Abstractions exists as an experiment in brutalist design coupled with chaotic-philo-concepts intended as an intentional rejection of the widely-accepted standards of visual communication embodied by the vast majority of internet pages. While traditional elements of color cohesion, typographical harmony, and 1 to 1.618 layout may not be present, the core functionality remains.

Theorems and Abstractions has no overt purpose other than to inspire some sort of thought about the visual monotony that surrounds each of us in our online and offline lives.
